The Best Movies according to Rik

My Top 3 Movies of all time

The Karate Kid

This movie is about a kid who gets bullied in China, after he moves there with his mother, gradually he learns kungfu and learns to protect himself.

Wall-E

This movie is set on a post-apocalyptic scene where there are no humans left, but robots. It is basically an innocent love story of a robot surviving on earth and one of the robots who come to visit earth.

Lucy

This story is about a human who somehow unlocks her brain's full potential and is able to use her brain efficiently on 100%
